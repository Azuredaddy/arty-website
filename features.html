Features That Make Arty Smart

🧠 Self-Learning AI

Arty observes your real tasks and learns how to automate them just by watching. No scripts or coding needed — simply show Arty what you do.

🎥 Screen + Input Tracking

Arty records screen captures, mouse clicks, and keyboard actions to build a step-by-step understanding of your workflows.

🔊 Voice Interaction

Arty listens and responds with speech, allowing voice-based guidance, confirmations, and intelligent clarification when confused.

📄 Smart Summarization

After watching a session, Arty creates a GPT-powered task summary that outlines the exact steps for future automation and replay.

🖱️ UI Element Detection

Arty captures button screenshots and labels them automatically, making it easy to click through interfaces even without APIs.

🔁 Task Replay

Arty replays tasks it has learned — clicking buttons, filling forms, or executing scripts exactly like a trained employee would.
