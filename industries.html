Industries Powered by Arty

From helpdesks to healthcare, Arty automates repetitive tasks and learns your workflows — boosting efficiency in every environment.

💻 Managed IT Service Providers (MSPs)

Arty automates password resets, ticket triage, and account provisioning — freeing up technicians for high-value tasks. Perfect for MSPs scaling without adding headcount.

🖥️ Internal IT Teams

Offload routine tasks like device setups, license audits, and user assistance. Arty learns once and handles it repeatedly without supervision.

📞 Call Centers & BPOs

Arty can monitor agent screens, learn how tickets are handled, and replicate task resolution steps — reducing training time and ticket load.

🏥 Healthcare Administration

Eliminate time-consuming tasks like onboarding users, managing accounts, and uploading documents. Arty adapts to EMRs and form-based portals.

🏛 Government & Education

Support repetitive portal work, policy rollout, and compliance tasks without compromising control. Deploy Arty on-premise for data security.

🚀 SaaS Startups

From onboarding workflows to managing trial requests, Arty ensures lean teams stay productive while scaling fast.

📂 Real Estate, Legal & Insurance

Form-heavy workflows, portal submissions, and compliance tasks are ideal for Arty’s visual automation — no API needed.
