The Mission Behind Arty

We’re building the ultimate AI-powered coworker — one that’s faster, smarter, tireless, and constantly improving.

Meet the Creator

Arty was created by a Microsoft Certified Expert with over 5 years of experience working in the MSP/Tech industry, specializing in Endpoint Management and Security for major Australian companies.

After witnessing firsthand the growing gap in L2 support, customer service, and call center staffing, the mission became clear: design an AI assistant that could watch, learn, and do the work — all without needing breaks, salaries, or hand-holding.

Arty helps businesses improve profitability by handling real work for less than the cost of a single human hire — and doing it better.
